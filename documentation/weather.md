# *[MolniOS](https://codeberg.org/al1h3n/molnios-install)* weather method:

All weather scripts and widgets use [wttr.in](https://github.com/chubin/wttr.in) data. The weather codes work in following way:
113 - Sunny
116 - Partly cloudy
119|122 - Cloudy or overcast
143|248|260 - Foggy
176|263|266|293|296 - Showers or light rain
179|227|230 - Snow or freezing rain
182|185|281|284|311|314|317|350|377|299|302|305|308 - Rain with thunder, heavy rain
200|386|389|392|395 - Thunderstorms
323|326|329|332|335|338|368|371|374 - Snowstorms or heavy snow
